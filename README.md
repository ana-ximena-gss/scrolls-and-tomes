# Scrolls & Tomes
Scrolls & Tomes is a browser-based social RPG where university students join guilds based on their academic major, complete knowledge challenges, and compete with other guilds.

# Project Description
Scrolls & Tomes is a fantasy-themed social web application designed for university students. The platform allows users to join guilds that represent different academic disciplines such as business, engineering, arts, and medicine. Once inside a guild, players can complete knowledge-based challenges related to their field of study to gain experience points and increase their rank.

The application combines social interaction and educational gameplay. Users will be able to communicate with members of their guild, compete against other guilds by answering questions from different academic areas to earn points, and track their progress through rankings and leaderboards. As players progress, they improve their rank and reputation within their guild while tracking their progress through leaderboards and achievements.
